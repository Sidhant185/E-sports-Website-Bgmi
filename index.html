Rules & Guidelines

⚔️ Tekken 7 Rules
▼

👾 General Rules:

  1. Controllers: All controllers will be provided by the organizing team. Personal controllers are not allowed unless approved by staff.
  2. Character Selection: Players may choose any character before the match begins. Once selected, character change is not allowed mid-match.
  3. No Cheats or Modifications: Strictly no mods, macros, or external aids.
  4. Fair Play: Unsportsmanlike behavior (taunting, pausing intentionally, verbal abuse) will lead to instant disqualification.
  5. Disconnect Rule: In case of a game crash or disconnect, the round may be restarted at the organizer's discretion.
  6. Judges' Decision: The organizing committee's decision will be final in all disputes.
  7. Knockout Format: Losing a match means elimination from the tournament — no retries.

📋 Note:

  • Only the participant (player) should fill this form for registration.
  • Players must report to the venue 10 minutes before their match.

⚡ Bring your A-game and fight your way to victory — only one champion will remain standing!

⚔️ Weapon Rules

✅ Allowed Weapons:

  • M416
  • AKM

❌ Restricted Weapons:

  • Any other gun (including special weapons like M24, AWM, etc.)
  • Using spawned sniper or heavy weapons between the containers is strictly prohibited.

🦺 Gameplay Restrictions

  • 🚫 No Sliding: Every player must turn off the slide feature before the match starts.
  • 🚫 No Level 3 Vests: Picking up Level 3 vests from the containers is not allowed.

🛑 Any player/team found violating the rules will face instant disqualification.

⚠️ Fair Play Reminder

All players are expected to maintain sportsmanship and respect event officials' decisions.

Any form of cheating, glitching, or exploiting bugs will result in immediate disqualification.
